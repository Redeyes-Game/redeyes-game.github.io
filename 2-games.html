Passman - secure passwords & pincodes keeper

PassMan is the easiest and most secure way to have all passwords and PIN codes at your fingertips.

PassMan store passwords in a secure internal storage on your iPhone and never sends them to the internet, neither to iCloud, nor to any other device. Your passwords won't be hijacked.

When Passwan is useful?

  • • at the cashier desk when you have to memorize you credit card PIN code
  • • in a hotel when you want to check you mail
  • • at work when you want to log into your personal account or social network
  • • at home when you need your credit card info to pay for airline ticket

Our application helps you to keep passwords, PIN codes and credit card information in an easily accessable yet super secure place. Owing to Touch ID and Passcode verification you can be sure that nonone can access your sensetive data even if he gets access to your device.

  • • Application stores passwords in a secure internal storage on your iPhone.
  • • It never sends your data to the internet.
  • • Application doesn't rely on Internet connection and work offline perfectly.
  • • To view your passwords and PIN codes you have to prove your identity by Passcode or Touch ID.
