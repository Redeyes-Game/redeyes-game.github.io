Bear's Dungeon - exciting adventures ahead, do not miss your chance

This game will tell you a wonderful story about Sadman, which was captured the evil bear in strange leather suites.

He lived himself and was unaware of the existence of these evil creatures. But one night they came to his house, dragged him from sleep warm and cozy bed and dragged to his lair.

Bears handcuffed him to a wall, so he did not run away, and put a strong guards. When Sadman came around in confusion and fear came at him, he realized that the stupid bear, whose head just fluff, just thrown the chain around, but Sadman has no hands... He saw helmet lying on the table and a small rocket. Without hesitation, he decided to escape, for that he has matured cunning plan. He lull bears his resignation, and sharp run, twitches, hook-alone table, grab a helmet and explode, there is strength! Forward!

Despite the fact that it is almost impossible to play, it is not the speed increases to infinity, as in other games, and if you got more than one minute, only decide on your abilities. Look at the leaderboard, only a few people can hold more than 2-3 minutes!

While controlling the hero using accelerometer you must dodge flying platforms, overcome obstacles and labyrinths.

Complexity of the game is constantly increasing. Only tough players can deal with it. Outlast as long as you can and prove your friends and the whole world that you're the best. To have some adrenaline buzz in the game we have added few bonuses that make the game more fun and exciting for a change.

Use them in the most difficult situations, because their number is limited.

Requirements

Bear's Dungeon requires Android 4.0.0+ or iOS 8+. I've tested TOW : Bears Dungeon on my Nexus 5 (Android 5.0-7.0), Nexus 5X (Android 5.0-8.0), Google Pixel (Android 8.1), Samsung S8 (Android 7.0 - 8.0), Nokia 8 Sirocco, iPhone 6S and iPhone8. All features available on all devices.

Note that it's not possible for me to test TOW : Bears Dungeon on every Android device out there, let alone in combination with different Android versions (or especially alternative ROMs like CyanogenMod).

Privacy policy

Bear's Dungeon will require access to internal storage to store game records and your progress.

Bear's Dungeon uses Unity Analytics to get information about amount of installs and countries. All data provided to google analytics is anonimous and not related to any personal information. Basicaly it is information about usage of the game, how long to you stay on the screen and etc. TOW : Bears Dungeon does not collect information that may be used to identify individuals.
